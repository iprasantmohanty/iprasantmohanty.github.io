About me

A Brief Introduction

I, Prasant Kumar Mohanty, am a Research Scholar, pursuing a Ph.D. degree in the Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), National Institute of Technology Meghalaya (NIT Meghalaya). Currently, I am working under the supervision of Dr. Diptendu Sinha Roy.I have more than 10 years of teaching experience at various Engineering Institutes in Odisha. Prior to that, I have completed my M.Tech degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Gandhi Institute of Engineering and Technology Gunupur, Odisha, and B.Tech degree in Information Technology from National Institute of Science and Technology Brahmapur, Odisha, India.


Area of Interests

My research interests include the use of Explanable AI methods in Smart City applications, Advanced Machine Learning Methods, and Data Science Engineering.
