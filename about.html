About me

A Brief Introduction

I, Ningombam Anandshree Singh, am a Dept. of Science and Technology (DST) -– INSPIRE Research Fellow, pursuing the Ph.D. degree in the Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (IIT Kharagpur). Currently, I am working under the supervision of Prof. Sudip Misra in Smart Wireless Applications and Networking (SWAN) Lab. Prior to that, I have completed my M.Tech degree and B.Tech degree in Computer Science and Engineering from National Institute of Technology Manipur, India. I am the M.Tech first position holder of our batch (2014-2016, NIT Manipur).


Area of Interests

My research interests include Internet of Things (IoT), Camera-Networks, Cloud Computing, Edge Computing, Artificial Intelligence, and Camera-Network-as-a-Service.
